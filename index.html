How to Pray the Rosary

The Rosary is twenty-one simple steps around a circle of beads — a walk through the life of Jesus with His Mother beside you. Since 2002, when St. John Paul II added the Luminous Mysteries in Rosarium Virginis Mariae, there have been four sets of Mysteries. Choose the set for today and let the guide carry you bead by bead: Joyful (Mondays & Saturdays), Sorrowful (Tuesdays & Fridays), Glorious (Wednesdays & Sundays), and Luminous (Thursdays) — the Mysteries of Light, which shine on Christ's public ministry from His Baptism to the Eucharist.
